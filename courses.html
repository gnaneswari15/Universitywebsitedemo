Undergraduate Courses

Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science

Focuses on software engineering, algorithms, and computational theory, preparing students for the tech industry.

Bachelor of Technology in Mechanical Engineering

Covers thermodynamics, mechanics, and material science, preparing students for careers in manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace industries.

Bachelor of Technology in Civil Engineering

Provides knowledge of structural engineering, construction management, and environmental systems for building infrastructure.

Bachelor of Technology in Electrical Engineering

Teaches electrical systems, electronics, and power distribution, preparing students for roles in power generation, automation, and electronics.

Postgraduate Courses

Master of Technology in Artificial Intelligence

Advanced study of AI algorithms, machine learning, and data analytics to prepare students for cutting-edge careers in AI technology.

Master of Technology in Structural Engineering

Focused on advanced structural analysis, material science, and construction practices for students aiming to become experts in structural design.

Master of Technology in Renewable Energy

Provides knowledge about sustainable energy solutions, focusing on solar, wind, and other renewable sources to meet global energy demands.
